Jeera Rice

Description

Quickest and easiest lunch rice recipes. Most Indian households love this recipe. It is easier to do and easy on your tummy. It digests very well.

Ingredients

  • Basmathi Rice - 1 Cup soaked for half an hour
  • Ghee - 3 table spoons
  • green chilies - 4
  • Jeera -- 1 tablespoon
  • Bay leaf - 1
  • Cardamom - 2 pods
  • Cloves - 2
  • Anise Seed - 1
  • Mint leaves - a few
  • Salt - as required
  • Water - 2 cups

Steps

  1. In a large pan, heat the ghee add jeera, cardamom, bay leaves, cloves, anise seeds and let it splatter
  2. Now the mint leaves and drained rice(which was soaked) and mix well
  3. Do NOT breakl the rice
  4. After sauteeing for 2 to 3 minutes, add water and salt and cook with lid for 5 to 7 minutes
  5. Do not let it get mushy, cook until the rice is perfect and stop
  6. Serve with a nice dal or eat just like that, tastes amazing.
